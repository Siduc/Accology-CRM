{# --- Fix leading zeros --- #}

2. Fix company numbers (leading zeros)

Excel often drops leading zeros (e.g. 8056337 instead of 08056337). Companies House needs the full 8-digit number, so those lookups fail as “not found”.

Safe to run more than once. Then re-run job creation for clients that failed.
